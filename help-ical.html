How to get your iCal URL

Each calendar provider gives you a secret .ics URL you can paste into Kallendar.

Google Calendar

Google Calendar

  1. Open calendar.google.com in a browser.
  2. In the left sidebar, find the calendar you want. Hover over it and click the ⋮ menu → Settings and sharing.
  3. Scroll down to Integrate calendar.
  4. Copy the link under Secret address in iCal format.
  5. Paste it into Kallendar's Subscribe to URL dialog.
Note: Use the Secret address, not the public URL — the secret one includes your private events.
Outlook

Microsoft Outlook

  1. Go to outlook.live.com/calendar.
  2. Right-click (or long-press) the calendar in the left sidebar → Sharing and permissions.
  3. Under Publish, set access to Can view all details, then click Publish.
  4. Copy the ICS link that appears.
  5. Paste it into Kallendar's Subscribe to URL dialog.
Note: For Microsoft 365 / work accounts the steps may differ — look for Publish calendar in Calendar settings.
Apple Calendar

Apple Calendar (iCloud)

  1. Go to icloud.com/calendar.
  2. Click the share icon 📡 next to the calendar name in the sidebar.
  3. Enable Public Calendar and copy the link shown.
  4. Replace webcal:// at the start of the URL with https://.
  5. Paste it into Kallendar's Subscribe to URL dialog.
Tip: The webcal:// → https:// swap is required — Kallendar fetches over HTTPS.
